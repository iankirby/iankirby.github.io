    Turkic languages

    Sakha

    Sakha (Yakut; ISO: sah) is a North Siberian Turkic language spoken in Sakha (Yakutia), a republic in the Russian Far East. With the closely related Dolgan language, it belongs to the North subbranch of the Siberian branch of Turkic Languages (Turkic > Common Turkic > Siberian > N. Siberian). It is spoken by an estimated 450,000 native speakers.
