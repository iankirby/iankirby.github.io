Wordle Ngrams

Compiled by Ian Kirby (Site, Twitter)

When playing Wordle, I started wondering about relative frequencies of letters by position in five letter words. So I messed around with a list of words from the hello-wordl GitHub repo. You can find the list of the words these figures are based on on this wordlist here, which includes 8938 words with five letters; list of words with columns for ngrams available in grams.csv. These may not be the same words in the dictionary used for daily wordle puzzle at powerlanguage.co.uk/wordle (the one that's been making the rounds on social media), but it will give a nice approximation either way.
